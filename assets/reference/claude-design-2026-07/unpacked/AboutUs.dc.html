About Huddle

Bringing families closer,
one community at a time.

Huddle is a privacy-first local family platform — built so parents can find trusted goods, activities, experts and each other, all in one warm place.

Est. 2024
Made in Oslo, Norway
Our story
One home for family life

Huddle started with a frustration every parent knows: family life is scattered across a dozen apps, group chats and marketplaces — none built for trust, safety or real community.

So we built one home for it: a marketplace for pre-loved and new goods, a place to find activities and events, and a way to connect with local families and experts — all designed with children's privacy and safety at the centre.
