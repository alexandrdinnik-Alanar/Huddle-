1. `02_MASTER_IDENTITY/` = identity source-of-truth.
2. `01_BRAND_GUIDELINES/02_APPROVED_REFERENCE_PNG/` = approved visual design reference.

## Identity
Never redraw, retype, distort, simplify, approximate or AI-regenerate the Huddle wordmark or Community Mark.

## Visual language
- Warm, light, tactile, editorial.
- Dominant: ivory, warm cream, soft white.
- Copper: signature accent.
- Sage: restrained.
- Deep forest green: controlled contrast only.
- Nodes = people; lines = relationships; clusters = community.

## Apparel
- Front: approved Huddle wordmark centered.
- Back: large approved Community Mark centered.
- Never add `Closer by Design` to apparel.

## Office
- Light walls dominate.
- Use light oak, pale stone/travertine, linen, brushed copper.
- Sage only as restrained accent.
- Startup scale, not oversized corporate architecture.
- Must read as a real office, not an apartment.

## Photography
- Warm Scandinavian daylight.
- Authentic, happy people.
- Prefer visible faces or natural engagement.
- Avoid excessive back-facing groups.
- Neutral clothing: ivory, cream, beige, oatmeal, taupe.

## Brand Guidelines editing
- Preserve approved visual appearance from PNG references.
- Rebuild text as editable text.
- Rebuild lines/shapes as editable vectors/shapes.
- Use approved identity masters.
- Keep photorealistic renders as raster where necessary.
- Never flatten the final editable source into one image per slide.

## Forbidden
- No duplicate guideline pages with the same content.
- Do not classify by filename alone; inspect content.
- No random dark corporate restyling.
- No invented logo variants.
