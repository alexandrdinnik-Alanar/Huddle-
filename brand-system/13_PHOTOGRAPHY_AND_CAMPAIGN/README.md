# 13 Photography and Campaign

Photography direction:
- Warm Scandinavian daylight.
- Authentic happy people and real interactions.
- Include different family stages and community roles.
- Prefer visible faces or natural engagement.
- Avoid excessive back-facing groups.
- Clothing palette: ivory, cream, oatmeal, beige, warm sand and light taupe.
- Avoid dominant green clothing.

Campaign language should be warm, human, direct and purposeful.
