# 12 Office and Environmental Branding

## Core environment
- Light walls dominate.
- Soft ivory plaster and warm white mineral paint.
- Light oak.
- Pale limestone or travertine.
- Natural linen and tactile woven textiles.
- Brushed copper accents.
- Soft sage only as a restrained accent.
- Clear or lightly frosted glass.

## Spatial rule
Startup scale: efficient, warm and flexible; not oversized corporate architecture. Office areas must read as real workspaces, not apartments.
