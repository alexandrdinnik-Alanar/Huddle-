## Accuracy rules

- Do not claim the product is launch-ready.
- Do not claim a route placeholder is a working feature.
- Do not claim Supabase, PostgreSQL, Vercel, monitoring, email, storage, or production environments are configured unless current evidence exists.
- Do not claim an approved Figma frame is implemented unless the route registry says `IMPLEMENTED`.
- Do not expose real secrets, credentials, internal tokens, or sensitive user data.
- Use `pnpm` commands only.
