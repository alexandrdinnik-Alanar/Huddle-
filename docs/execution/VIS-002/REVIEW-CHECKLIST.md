# VIS-002 D1 Product-Owner Review Checklist

Use the same questions for all three concepts.

## 1. Immediate reaction

- Which direction feels most recognizably Huddle?
- Which direction feels least template-like?
- Which direction creates the strongest trust without feeling institutional?

## 2. Public discovery fit

- Does the homepage make local family discovery feel useful and desirable?
- Is the search/discovery model understandable in a few seconds?
- Does the activity presentation support real family decisions?

## 3. Brand character

- Is the direction mature without becoming cold?
- Is it warm without becoming childish?
- Is it modern without relying on short-lived visual effects?
- Could the visual language belong to another company unchanged?

## 4. Product scalability

- Can this language extend to Auth, App and Admin surfaces?
- Can dense product information remain clear?
- Will Norwegian and English content fit comfortably?
- Does mobile retain the concept rather than collapse into a generic stack?

## 5. Accessibility and restraint

- Is the hierarchy readable?
- Are interactive elements obvious enough?
- Is motion purposeful and optional?
- Are decorative details helping rather than competing?
