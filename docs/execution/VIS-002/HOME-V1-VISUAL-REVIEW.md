# Huddle Home v1 — Browser Visual Review

Review source: Playwright full-page captures generated by CI at 1440, 1024, 768 and 390 px.

Status: the route renders successfully at every target width and has no detected horizontal page overflow. The PR remains Draft because visual corrections and final photography are still required.

## What works

- The official Huddle logo and icon render correctly.
- The family-platform hierarchy is clear: planning, activities, community, services, then marketplace.
- The activities, community and services sections use different spatial compositions instead of one repeated card row.
- Marketplace remains a compact secondary module.
- Tablet and mobile layouts preserve all major sections.
- The app banner and desktop footer complete the page coherently.

## P0 visual corrections before approval

### 1. Desktop hero content collision

The large editorial heading, supporting paragraph, CTA group and local-pick card compete for the same lower-left area. The supporting copy is partially obscured in the 1440 capture.

Required correction:

- increase the desktop hero height or reduce the display scale;
- move the local-pick card onto the photography area;
- keep the supporting paragraph and both CTAs fully visible;
- preserve clear space around the family faces and today-plan card.

### 2. Mobile navigation clarity

The desktop navigation disappears below 900 px, leaving only the logo and two utility icons. The primary product areas are no longer directly discoverable.

Required correction:

- add a clear mobile menu control or a compact horizontally scrollable primary navigation;
- retain access to Discover, Plan, Community, Services and Marketplace;
- keep touch targets at least 44 px where practical.

### 3. Mobile footer density

The two-column footer produces very small links and weak grouping on 390 px.

Required correction:

- use collapsed groups or a simplified mobile footer;
- keep brand, support, privacy, language and essential navigation visible;
- remove nonessential repeated links from the initial mobile view.

## P1 visual corrections

### Photography and semantic fit

The external editorial placeholders are technically high resolution but several images do not match their labels:

- some activity and service cards reuse unrelated photography;
- marketplace product imagery does not consistently match product titles;
- community imagery needs a more coherent Oslo and local-family direction.

Replace the placeholder set with a controlled Huddle photo pack before launch.

### Text hierarchy

Some card labels and footer links are too small in the compact desktop, tablet and mobile captures. Increase the minimum readable size and strengthen contrast on image overlays.

### Trust ribbon

The mobile horizontal ribbon is functional but does not clearly indicate that more items can be scrolled. Add a visible continuation cue or simplify the mobile set.
