# Huddle Home v1 — Production Photo Pack Requirements

The current homepage uses temporary external editorial photography. Before launch, replace it with a controlled Huddle-owned or properly licensed photo pack.

## Required assets

### Hero

- one horizontal master, minimum 3200 × 1800 px;
- real family in a warm, natural environment;
- clear negative space for editorial copy;
- safe crop zones for 1440, 1024, 768 and 390 px;
- faces must remain unobstructed by the today-plan card.

### Activities

- Art & Imagination — portrait or near-square;
- Outdoor Adventures — wide landscape;
- Movement & Sport — portrait;
- Music & Dance — portrait;
- onboarding/support image — compact landscape.

### Community

- one large local-family gathering image;
- three supporting local-group images;
- documentary rather than stock-advertising direction;
- Oslo or recognizably Nordic context where possible.

### Trusted Services

- childcare and preschool;
- wellbeing or family counselling;
- tutoring and learning;
- party or event planning.

Each image must clearly match its service label.

### Marketplace

- wooden balance bike;
- children’s daypack;
- wooden blocks;
- insulated bottle.

Use isolated or carefully staged product photography on light neutral surfaces. Product identity must match the card text exactly.

### App banner

- one horizontal family-at-home image with room for the app panel;
- no duplicated hero photograph.

## Visual direction

- natural light;
- warm neutrals compatible with Warm Cream, Sand Beige and Copper Clay;
- authentic family interaction;
- no exaggerated advertising smiles;
- no generic corporate stock aesthetic;
- consistent grain, temperature and contrast across the set.

## Delivery

For every asset provide:

- high-resolution master;
- optimized AVIF or WebP;
- desktop crop;
- tablet crop;
- mobile crop;
- descriptive alt text;
- source, ownership and license record.
