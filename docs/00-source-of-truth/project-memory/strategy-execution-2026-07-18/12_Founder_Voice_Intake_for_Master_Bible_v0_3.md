# Founder Voice Intake — Master Project Bible v0.3

Date: 2026-07-18  
Purpose: collect Alexander’s founder voice for Section 2 and future presentations  
Status: waiting for founder input

## Why this is needed

The Master Project Bible v0.2 is strategically strong, but Section 2 still needs more founder voice.

Right now the origin story explains the project professionally. For team, investor, grant, municipality and public-facing materials, it also needs a more personal layer:

- why this project matters to Alexander;
- what real-life frustration or observation started it;
- why Huddle should exist now;
- what kind of company Huddle should become;
- why families and children are not just a market, but the centre of the mission.

## How to answer

You can answer in rough notes, mixed Russian/English, voice-style text, bullet points or emotional fragments. It does not need to be polished. The goal is to capture truth, not perfect wording.

## Founder questions

### 1. The first spark

What was the first moment when you thought: “This problem needs to be solved”?

Prompts:

- Was it connected to your own family?
- Was it something you saw in Norway/Oslo?
- Was it frustration with how parents search for activities, goods or support?
- Was it connected to children spending too much time in digital spaces?

### 2. The emotional reason

Why do you personally care about this project?

Prompts:

- What feels unfair or broken in the current family experience?
- What kind of relief do you want parents to feel?
- What kind of world do you want children to grow up in?

### 3. The family problem in your own words

If you had to explain the problem to another parent in one minute, what would you say?

### 4. The founder belief

What do you believe that others may underestimate?

Examples:

- Families need more than search.
- Parents need trust before they act.
- Children need real-world alternatives, not just screen restrictions.
- Local community can be rebuilt through better infrastructure.

### 5. Why Huddle is different

Why should Huddle exist instead of another marketplace, app, event calendar or Facebook group?

### 6. Why Norway / Oslo first

Why do you believe Norway and Oslo are the right starting point?

### 7. The company you want to build

What kind of company should Huddle become?

Prompts:

- premium and trusted;
- family-first;
- socially useful;
- commercially strong;
- privacy-safe;
- design-led;
- global from a Norwegian foundation.

### 8. What Huddle must never become

What would be unacceptable?

Examples:

- a manipulative social network;
- a child-data business;
- a low-trust marketplace;
- a noisy parent forum;
- a company that sells safety as marketing but does not protect families.

### 9. The sentence you want people to remember

After reading about Huddle, what should people feel or remember?

### 10. Founder quote

Write 2–5 rough sentences that could become a founder quote.

Example format:

> “I started Huddle because…”
