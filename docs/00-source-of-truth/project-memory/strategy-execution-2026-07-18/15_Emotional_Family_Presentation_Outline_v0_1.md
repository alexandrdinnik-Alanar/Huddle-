Audience: crowdfunding, community, partners, public talks, team inspiration  
External status: not ready until founder voice and QA

## Purpose

This is the emotional version of Huddle.

It should not feel like an investor deck. It should help people feel why Huddle matters.

The core emotional idea:

Families should not have to navigate local life alone.

## Tone

Warm, honest, human, calm, hopeful.

Avoid:

- exaggerated startup language;
- fear-based parenting language;
- manipulating emotion;
- too many numbers;
- technical detail.
