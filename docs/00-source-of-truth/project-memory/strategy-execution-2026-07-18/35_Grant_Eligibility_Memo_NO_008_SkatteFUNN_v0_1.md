## Decision recommendation

Recommendation: prepare after incorporation and R&D project definition.

Fit for Huddle: high conditional fit if Huddle defines a genuine R&D project and has a Norwegian tax-liable company structure.

SkatteFUNN is not a normal cash grant. It is a tax deduction scheme for R&D costs.

## Official source summary

Official URL:

https://www.forskningsradet.no/skattefunn/

Official page status checked on 2026-07-18:

- SkatteFUNN is a rights-based tax deduction scheme for Norwegian companies.
- Companies can apply for a tax deduction of 19% of costs for a research and development project.
- The scheme is administered by the Research Council of Norway in cooperation with the Norwegian Tax Administration.
- Companies of all sizes, industries and company forms can apply if registered in Brønnøysundregisteret and taxable to Norway.
- The official page advises submitting early in the calendar year and states that applications submitted before 1 September can be guaranteed processing for the current year.

## Huddle fit

Potentially strong fit if the project is framed around real R&D:

- privacy-preserving recommendation or matching logic;
- family-context data minimisation;
- child/family safety-by-design;
- explainability of recommendations;
- safe aggregation for public-value insight;
- new method for local family participation discovery.

Weak fit if it is only:

- website development;
- routine app development;
- visual design;
- marketing;
- sales/outreach;
- ordinary business operations.

## Budget fit

SkatteFUNN requires a defined R&D project and proper project accounting.

Potential Huddle cost areas to evaluate:

- product/tech R&D work;
- prototype experiments;
- data/privacy architecture exploration;
- technical uncertainty work;
- external R&D assistance, if eligible and documented.

Costs to keep out unless clearly eligible:

- marketing;
- ordinary sales/outreach;
- regular operations;
- brandbook/design-only work;
- non-R&D content production;
- unsupported founder time assumptions.

## Relation to Innovation Norway and other support

Innovation Norway official pages require disclosure of other public support and note that support can be reduced if SkatteFUNN covers the same project costs.

Therefore, Huddle must maintain separate cost mapping:

- which costs are planned for SkatteFUNN;
- which costs are planned for Innovation Norway;
- which costs are investor-funded;
- which costs are founder-funded;
- which costs are not eligible for public support.
