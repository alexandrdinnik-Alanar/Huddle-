Tone: clear, warm, non-technical, human.

## Slide 2 — The problem

Title:

Family life is too fragmented.

Copy:

Parents search across websites, chats, groups, calendars, marketplaces and recommendations just to understand what is nearby, suitable and trustworthy.

## Slide 3 — The question families ask

Title:

What can we do nearby — safely, affordably and with people we can trust?

Copy:

This is the everyday question Huddle is built to answer.

## Slide 4 — What Huddle is

Title:

One trusted doorway into local family life.

Copy:

Huddle helps families discover activities, events, trusted organisers, local groups, family services and useful children’s goods.

## Slide 5 — How it works

Title:

From search to participation.

Copy:

Family context → discover → understand → trust → act → attend → return.

## Slide 6 — Built around families

Title:

Relevant to your child, controlled by adults.

Copy:

Huddle is designed around adult-managed family context. Children should not become public searchable profiles by default.

Claim IDs:

C-015, C-017.

## Slide 7 — Activities and events

Title:

Find what fits.

Copy:

## Slide 8 — Marketplace and reuse

Title:

Useful family goods, new and pre-owned.

Copy:

## Slide 9 — Experts and services

Title:

Support when families need it.

Title:

Belonging without noise.
