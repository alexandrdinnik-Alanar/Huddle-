## Applicant route

The project owner must be registered in the Norwegian Register of Business Enterprises and have economic activity in Norway.

The project owner must carry out the project with at least one collaborating Norwegian company partner or procure commissioned research from at least one R&D provider.

Current Huddle blocker:

Huddle’s planned operating/applicant company is ALANAR AS, not yet incorporated. `[C-002]` `[C-003]`

Therefore Huddle is not ready to be project owner until legal/applicant setup is confirmed.

## Huddle fit

Potentially strong fit if Huddle develops a true R&D project around:

- privacy-preserving family-context matching;
- explainable recommendation logic for family participation;
- data minimisation and safety-by-design in child/family platforms;
- aggregated, non-identifying local participation insights;
- circular family equipment/reuse models with measurable environmental benefit;
- trusted organiser data quality and participation prediction.

Weak fit if framed as:

- building the first website;
- building normal marketplace functionality;
- doing marketing research;
- launching a pilot without R&D uncertainty;
- ordinary software implementation.

Privacy-preserving family participation intelligence for local community infrastructure

Core R&D question:

Can adult-controlled family context and privacy-preserving aggregation improve relevant local participation without exposing individual child or family data?

Possible R&D work packages:

1. Data minimisation model for family participation.
2. Explainable family-context matching model.
3. Privacy-preserving aggregate insight model for municipalities/partners.
4. Pilot measurement methodology.
5. Trust & Safety risk model.
6. Prototype and experimental validation.

## Budget fit

Official cost categories include:

- payroll and indirect costs;
- procurement of R&D;
- equipment;
- other project costs.

The official call notes that it does not support activities not directly related to R&D implementation or exploitation of R&D results, including market research, marketing, product completion and unsupported unpaid work.

Funding can cover up to 50% of company costs under the call, with the final percentage determined in the grant letter.

## Required partners

Huddle must identify either:

- at least one independent collaborating Norwegian company partner; or
- at least one R&D provider.

Potential partner types to explore:

- Norwegian research organisation with competence in privacy, children/families, recommender systems, participation or civic data;
- municipality/public-sector innovation partner, if allowed in the project structure;
- independent Norwegian company partner in family services, reuse/circular economy, activity supply or privacy technology.
