## Summary

Four priority grant/funding routes were checked against live official sources and converted into first eligibility memos.

## Route ranking

| Route | Fit now | Recommended timing | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| Innovation Norway Oppstartstilskudd 1 | High | Wave 1 | Best fit for early market validation and paying-market clarification. |
| Innovation Norway Oppstartstilskudd 2 | High conditional | Wave 2 | Stronger after 90-day validation, MVP scope and technical/business uncertainty are clearer. |
| Research Council IPN Industry & Services 2026 | High conditional | Wave 2/3 | Requires real R&D project plus partner/R&D-provider structure. |
| SkatteFUNN | High conditional | After incorporation/R&D definition | Useful tax-deduction route, but needs company setup and R&D project accounting. |

## Main conclusion

The cleanest immediate route is Innovation Norway Oppstartstilskudd 1.

Reason:

- it matches Huddle’s current pre-validation stage;
- it is small enough to structure quickly;
- it can support market validation rather than forcing Huddle into a premature large R&D application;
- it does not require the same level of R&D partner structure as IPN.

## Common blockers across routes

1. ALANAR AS is not yet incorporated. `[C-002]` `[C-003]`
2. Finance/controller owner is not assigned.
3. Grant budgets are not yet populated with real numbers.
4. R&D scope is not yet defined.
5. Privacy/legal/child-safety review is not complete. `[C-016]`
6. Public-impact evidence does not yet exist. `[C-020]`

## Application-risk rules

Do not submit any route until:

- applicant authority is confirmed;
- project scope is funder-specific;
- budget follows funder cost rules;
- any overlap with SkatteFUNN or other public support is disclosed and controlled;
- claims are checked;
- founder approves the application.
